About

I’m a Vancouver based UX and UI designer, illustrator, animator and 3d modeller with a background in technology, crypto and fintech. In the digital age, the ability to digest massive amounts of data very quickly but also in a seamless and fun way is an integral part of any business, software and life. User experience goes hand and hand with efficient user interfaces that have subtle colour psychology, precise organization of components, and optimization of massive data. These are skills that compliment my illustrations and animations that are fun, unique, memorable and will evoke the emotions your business wants to convey to its consumers. So, create a brand with me, update your company’s existing style guide with creative changes, or dive into your data and market research to develop your business into something truly inventive and interactive.
